# Auralis AI Academy Registry

This page is the public-facing graduate and certificate registry for the academy.

It is designed to help owners quickly understand:

- who has graduated
- what program was completed
- which cohort the graduate belongs to
- whether the certificate is active, restricted, inactive, or revoked
