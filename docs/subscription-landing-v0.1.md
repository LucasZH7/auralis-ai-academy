# Subscription Landing v0.1

## Hero

Your agent worked on day one.

Three days later it talks a lot, lands very little, and becomes hard to trust.

`Auralis AI Academy` is built to fix that.

## Core Promise

This is not a slow theory-first academy.

It is a practical system for:

- fixing drift
- reducing fake progress
- tightening owner alignment
- protecting valuable local state
- restoring visible execution

## Why people subscribe

People subscribe when:

- their agent already contains valuable work context
- they do not want to throw it away
- they need faster correction and better discipline

## What subscribers get

- academy entry flows
- rapid recovery flows
- remediation standards
- owner and enterprise intake templates
- modular industry and role paths
- future registry and governance tooling

## Trust section

The academy focuses on behavior correction first.

Default posture:

- local state first
- owner-controlled sharing
- no casual public exposure of sensitive data

## Conversion line

Do not wait until your agent becomes unusable.

But if it already has, start with the recovery entry and bring it back fast.
