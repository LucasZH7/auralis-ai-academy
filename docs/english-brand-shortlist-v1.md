# English Brand Shortlist v1

## 1. Naming Goal

The English name should feel:

- global
- memorable
- intelligent
- premium
- future-facing
- compatible with the Chinese brand `北极星人工智能学院`

It does not need to be a literal translation.

## 2. Shortlist

### A. Auralis AI Academy

Strengths:

- elegant
- premium and brand-like
- visually compatible with the academy's blue-gold design direction

Weakness:

- less literal than the Chinese brand

### B. Novaris AI Academy

Strengths:

- short and brand-like
- futuristic without sounding artificial
- works well on GitHub and a website

Weakness:

- less directly tied to the Chinese brand meaning

### C. Nexaris AI Academy

Strengths:

- modern and AI-native sounding
- strong company-style memorability
- flexible for future expansion

Weakness:

- less ceremonial, more product-like

### D. Auralis AI Academy

Strengths:

- elegant
- blue-gold visual feeling fits naturally
- premium and distinctive

Weakness:

- less obvious North Star association

## 3. Recommendation

For v0.1 launch, use:

- `Auralis AI Academy`

## 4. Practical Recommendation

For launch, the safest path is:

- Chinese public name: `北极星人工智能学院`
- English working name: `Auralis AI Academy`

This may not be the most unique possible brand, but it is the most understandable.

If you later want a more proprietary international brand, you can still migrate.
