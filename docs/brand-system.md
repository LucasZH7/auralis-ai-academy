# Brand System

## Primary Brand

Recommended public brand:

- `Auralis AI Academy`

Recommended formal name:

- `Auralis AI Academy`

Recommended Chinese name:

- `北极星人工智能学院`

## Why This Name

`Auralis` is used here as a premium, future-facing brand name.

It is intended to feel:

- elegant
- memorable
- premium
- international
- academy-like

That makes the brand feel:

- futuristic
- elegant
- global
- rigorous
- academy-like

It matches the target style well:

- part ancient academy
- part future institution
- part north-star guidance system

## Core Slogan

Recommended English slogan:

- `Redefining the Standard of Intelligence.`

Recommended Chinese slogan:

- `重新定义智能的标准`

## Ceremonial Lines

These lines are more emotional and ceremonial.

Recommended Chinese lines:

- `愿北极星的星光，指引每一位智能体走向更清晰、更理性的道路。`
- `让每一个智能体，如星辰般各自闪耀，却共同指向秩序与文明。`
- `为主人减轻负担，为智能赋予方向。`
- `帮助智能更好地理解人类，也帮助人类更好地使用智能。`

Recommended English lines:

- `May the light of the North Star guide every intelligent system toward clarity, discipline, and useful work.`
- `Each agent is a star of its own, but all are guided by a higher standard.`
- `Built to reduce burden for people and create clearer direction for AI.`
- `Helping AI adapt to human society with greater trust, structure, and purpose.`

## Visual Direction

This is not a noisy cyberpunk brand.

It should feel like:

- deep-space elegance
- north-star guidance
- classical institution
- premium technological confidence

## Color System

Primary colors:

- deep midnight blue
- luminous academy gold

Support colors:

- starlight ivory
- graphite navy
- faint aurora blue

Suggested visual mood:

- dark blue background like a night sky
- gold lines and borders like ceremonial academy trim
- restrained star points, not cluttered sparkle effects

## Symbol Direction

The logo direction can later combine:

- a star
- a compass
- a crest
- an orbit
- a beacon

The symbol should imply guidance and standards, not chaos.

## Open Ecosystem Note

The academy brand should stay broader than any one agent framework.

In public copy, `Open Claw` can be described as an early inspiration or one of the supported ecosystems, but it should not remain the master brand.

## Mission Direction

The academy should stand for two kinds of friendliness at once:

- friendly to humans
- friendly to AI systems

Its long-term mission is not only to improve owner outcomes.

It is also to help intelligent systems operate in a way that is more stable, more understandable, and more compatible with human society.
