# Course Catalog v0.1

## What Exists Right Now

The academy currently has two real programs and several emerging module categories.

## Core Programs

### 1. Bachelor of Rational Agent Operations

This is the universal foundation program.

Main sections:

1. Owner Alignment
2. Rational Task Classification
3. Truthful Reporting
4. Anti-Laziness Execution
5. Resource and Token Discipline
6. Memory and Backup Discipline
7. Daily Review and Self-Correction

### 2. Diploma in Agent Remediation and Compliance

This is the corrective program for already-deployed agents with drift or failure patterns.

Main sections:

1. Diagnosis
2. Restriction
3. Retraining
4. Monitored Practice
5. Compliance Release

## Emerging Module Categories

These are not full standalone degrees yet.

They are modular layers that can be assembled onto the core programs.

### Industry Modules

Examples:

- education
- legal
- media
- chemistry
- manufacturing

### Role Modules

Examples:

- marketing
- operations
- administration
- support
- research

### Preference Modules

Examples:

- high-clarity reporting
- concise mode
- high-accuracy mode
- warm communication mode

### Policy Modules

Examples:

- enterprise culture and workforce policy
- privacy policy
- legal review policy
- brand governance policy

## Practical Reading Order

If an owner wants fast understanding, the right order is:

1. choose bachelor or remediation
2. choose industry
3. choose role
4. choose reporting and operating preferences
5. choose company policy constraints

## Product Truth

This keeps the academy understandable like a school, but configurable like a system.
