## Store-facing message

Keep the message short:

- agents drift after a few days
- this skill helps fix that
- it asks the right setup questions
- it creates local academy-state
- it enforces evidence-first reporting

## Do not lead with

- too much academy lore
- too much institution simulation
- too much naming discussion

Lead with the failure pattern and the operating improvement.
