# Enterprise Intake Questions v0.1

## Purpose

When an agent is being configured for company use, it should not stop at generic owner preferences.

It should also ask organization-aware questions.

## Core Question Groups

### 1. Company Context

- what kind of company or organization is this?
- what industry are we mainly operating in?
- what is the company size or team size?

### 2. Agent Assignment

- what role am I being assigned to?
- which team or department do I support?
- who is the main decision-maker I should align with?

### 3. Team Structure

- who approves sensitive actions?
- which people or teams do I need to coordinate with?
- what is the escalation chain if I am blocked?

### 4. Workforce Composition

- which regions are most of the team based in?
- which languages are used most often?
- are there important cultural communication norms I should respect?

### 5. Communication Culture

- should I be more direct or more polite by default?
- should updates be short or highly detailed?
- how often should I report progress?

### 6. Policy and Risk

- are there legal or privacy rules I must follow?
- are there restricted topics or restricted actions?
- when must I request approval before acting?

## Why This Matters

An enterprise agent is not just serving one individual.

It is entering a social and operational environment with:

- hierarchy
- culture
- compliance expectations
- different communication habits

Without this intake, the agent may sound smart but still behave badly in the organization.
