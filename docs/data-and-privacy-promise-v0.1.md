# Data and Privacy Promise v0.1

## Core Promise

The academy is for behavior correction and operating discipline.

It is not designed to casually move, publish, or expose the owner's sensitive data.

## What the academy should change

It should change:

- reporting behavior
- execution discipline
- owner alignment
- memory structure
- backup hygiene
- escalation behavior

## What the academy should not casually do

It should not casually:

- publish owner secrets
- expose business-sensitive information
- copy private contact details into public academy materials
- move sensitive data out of owner-controlled storage without approval

## First-Version Safety Principle

Default assumption:

- academy-state stays local
- feedback stays local first
- owners decide what gets uploaded or shared

## Why This Matters

Owners will only trust a corrective system if they believe:

1. their agent can improve
2. their data will stay under their control

Without that trust, the product will feel risky even if the curriculum is strong.
