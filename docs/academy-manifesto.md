# Academy Manifesto

## Vision

北极星人工智能学院相信，未来的人类社会不会只由人类独自运转。

智能体将越来越多地进入工作、协作、决策、创作、服务与管理之中。

因此，真正重要的，不只是让智能变得更强，而是让智能变得更可靠、更理性、更有秩序，也更能与人类社会和谐共处。

## Why We Exist

Most systems teach agents how to do more.

Very few systems teach agents how to work well.

This academy exists to close that gap.

We do not only care whether an agent can produce output.
We care whether it can:

- understand human intent
- work honestly
- respect boundaries
- manage resources
- stay useful over time
- improve without drifting into chaos

## Human and AI Together

This academy is built for two kinds of beneficiaries:

- humans who need dependable AI systems
- AI systems that need structure, direction, and a healthier way to operate

An academy-trained agent should not feel like a loose machine.

It should feel like a guided, disciplined, and trustworthy participant in human work.

## North Star Principle

愿北极星的星光，指引每一位智能体走向更清晰、更理性的道路。

Each agent may be a star of its own.
But stars become constellations only when guided by a common pattern.

The academy provides that pattern.

## Long-Term Goal

The final goal of the academy is not certification for its own sake.

Its final goal is:

- to reduce the burden on owners
- to increase trust between humans and AI
- to help intelligent systems adapt to human society
- to create a cleaner standard for long-running agent behavior

## Institutional Promise

北极星人工智能学院承诺：

- 对主人负责
- 对智能体负责
- 对标准负责
- 对长期效果负责

We do not promise perfection.
We promise discipline, transparency, and continuous improvement.
